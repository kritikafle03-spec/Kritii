Your Luxury Home at the Foot of Mount Everest

Our Story: Where Luxury Meets the Highest Peak

The Snow Peak Mountain Resort is built near the magnificent MOUNT EVEREST. We wanted to create a place where the mountain's amazing beauty meets the very best in comfort and style.

This resort is a special project, made to be a safe, warm, and beautiful base for people who love this powerful mountain. We use smart, modern building design to keep you warm and safe, even with the extreme weather outside. Our main goal is to give you a private, peaceful, and super-luxurious stay.

World-Class Food

Eating here is an adventure. Our main restaurant, "The Skyward Table", prepares high-quality food from the best ingredients, brought in fresh. You can also relax at the stylish "Aura Bar" for a drink or grab a hot coffee and a pastry at "The Glacier Cafe". All places have beautiful mountain views.

Relax and Recharge

Our Sanctuary Spa is built into the mountain. It includes a warm indoor pool and special treatments designed to help you relax and feel great after your journey. We have special rooms and sessions to help you adjust to the altitude easily.
